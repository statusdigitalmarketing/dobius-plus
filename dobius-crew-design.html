Live

The pulse of the whole operation — every agent, right now. Bars dance while it works, breathe amber while it waits on you, go flat when it sleeps.

Amber is the only loud color rule

Working is calm green, sleeping is an outline, errors are quiet red rows — but "waiting on you" gets the tinted ticket, the pulsing dot, and the badge. If nothing needs you, the page is nearly monochrome.

Answer where you're asked P3

Every ticket shows the exact command, diff, or question — never a paraphrase — with Approve / Edit / Respond / Deny inline. "Always allow" is how the queue gets quieter every week.

Cost lives on results, not rows rule

The roster shows who needs you; dollars appear on run summaries and the titlebar day rollup. Triage first, accounting second.

The pips are a promise P2

"pings 2/4" is the crew's daily interruption budget. When it's spent, everything demotes to the morning briefing. A crew you trust is one that shuts up.

Icons are lucide — the app's real icon set. Renders in Geist inside the app. Phases: P1 identity/memory · P2 heartbeats/briefing · P3 decision queue · P4 channels · P5 unified board with terminal agents. Architecture: plans/CREW-DESIGN.md.
